Executive Summary

Kelly Fischer is an accomplished executive professional whose career spans municipal government, public education, and private sector leadership. With a precision-driven, people-first philosophy, she has consistently elevated the organizations she has served—managing complex governance structures, confidential personnel matters, and high-stakes institutional operations.

In her current role as Executive Assistant to the Superintendent and School Board at Mora Public Schools, Kelly operates at the highest level of institutional trust, serving as a de facto chief of staff and acting lead in the Superintendent's absence. Her scope of responsibility encompasses district policy interpretation, board governance, elections administration, HR operations, audit preparation, and financial compliance.

Kelly brings a rare combination of technical command—financial systems, HR platforms, compliance frameworks—and human leadership: warmth, composure under pressure, and a deep commitment to the communities she serves.
